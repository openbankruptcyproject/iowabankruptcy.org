Bankruptcy Filing for Waukee Residents

Waukee is served by the Southern District of Iowa (S.D. Iowa). The courthouse is located at 123 E. Walnut St, Des Moines, IA 50309.

Iowa exemptions: UNLIMITED homestead (must be less than 1/2 acre in a city or 40 acres elsewhere), $7,000 vehicle. Iowa is one of the most debtor-friendly states in the country for homestead protection.

Whether you file Chapter 7 or Chapter 13 depends on your income, assets, and financial goals.

Chapter 7 vs. Chapter 13

Chapter 7

  • Eliminates most unsecured debt
  • Takes 3-4 months
  • Must pass means test
  • Filing fee: $338

Chapter 13

  • 3-5 year repayment plan
  • Keep all your property
  • No means test required
  • Filing fee: $313

Frequently Asked Questions

Where do Waukee residents file for bankruptcy?

Waukee residents file through the Southern District of Iowa (S.D. Iowa). The courthouse is at 123 E. Walnut St, Des Moines, IA 50309.

What exemptions apply in Waukee?

Waukee residents use Iowa state exemptions: Unlimited homestead, $7,000 vehicle, $1,000 wildcard.

How much does bankruptcy cost in Waukee?

Costs are the same across Iowa. Chapter 7 filing fee is $338, Chapter 13 is $313. Attorney fees typically range from $1,000 to $2,500 for Chapter 7 and $3,000 to $5,000 for Chapter 13.
